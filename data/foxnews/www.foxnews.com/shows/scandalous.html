      Scandalous

      Narrator

      Collapse / Expand

      • Bruce McGill

        Narrated by acclaimed actor Bruce McGill, “Scandalous” features interviews with more than 45 people who were involved on both sides of the investigation and subsequent impeachment trial, to get an honest and detailed account of one the most intense political and legal stories in American history. Filmed in a cinematic style, the series will feature archival footage and records as well as never-before seen photos of the investigation.

      About the Show

      "Scandalous" is a historical documentary-style series that chronicles the sequence of events that enveloped Washington and ultimately led to the impeachment of President Bill Clinton during the 1990’s.
