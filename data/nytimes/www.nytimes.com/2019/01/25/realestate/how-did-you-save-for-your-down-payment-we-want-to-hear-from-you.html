How Did You Save for Your Down Payment? We Want to Hear From You.

Tell us how much you set aside and how you stayed motivated.

By Christina Caron

  • Jan. 25, 2019

Saving enough money for a down payment is one of the biggest barriers to homeownership, particularly in expensive cities like New York.

We want to hear from those who have recently purchased a home or are planning to purchase one this year — especially those who did not receive help from relatives. How did you do it? How did you manage to save such a large amount of money?

Tell us below.

Christina Caron is a general assignment reporter. Before coming to The Times, she spent a decade working for broadcast news organizations, and earned a master's degree from the Missouri School of Journalism. @cdcaron
