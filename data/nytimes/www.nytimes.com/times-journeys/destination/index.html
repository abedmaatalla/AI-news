North America

You already know about Africa’s remarkable landscapes and wildlife. But our trips go beyond the Africa of holiday brochures. Join us as we travel to the heart of its buzzing, historic cities and explore the cultural nuances of its hugely varied countries.

Home to the majority of the world’s population, Asia offers a feast of cultures, peoples and history. Explore the vast enigma that is modern-day China, or trace the history and influence of the Hindu and Buddhist beliefs that permeate India and Southeast Asia.

Europe is a dazzlingly diverse continent, from the green hills of Ireland in the west to the grand churches of Russia in the east. Our tours delve into its fascinating past and take a close look at its politically complex present.

It may be “home,” but it’s still full of surprises. Stretching from the Arctic cool of northern Canada to the sweltering heat of Mexico, our continent is as rich in culture, history and geography as any region in the world.

From the beautiful architecture and geographical diversity of Colombia to the fascinating wildlife of the Galápagos, South America delights and surprises you at every turn. Join us for in-depth, expert-led explorations of some of the region’s most compelling places.
